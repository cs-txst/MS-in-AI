# Program Faculty & Research Expertise

---

The faculty in the Department of Computer Science are a group of accomplished researchers, dedicated educators, and leaders in their respective fields. Students in the M.S. in Artificial Intelligence program will learn from and collaborate with experts who are actively pushing the boundaries of AI.

Our faculty's research is supported by prestigious federal and industry grants from organizations like the National Science Foundation (NSF), the Department of Defense (DOD), the Department of Energy (DOE), Google, and Meta.

### Core Research Areas in AI

Our department has a strong concentration of faculty with expertise across the entire spectrum of artificial intelligence. Key research areas include:

*   **Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning:** Developing novel algorithms and theoretical models for machine and deep learning.
*   **Generative AI and Deep Learning:** Focusing on models that can create new content and the multi-layered neural networks that power modern AI.
*   **Ethical and Responsible AI:** Investigating the critical concepts of fairness, bias, explainability, and the societal impact of AI systems.
*   **Computer Vision and Image Processing:** Enabling computers to interpret and understand visual information from images and videos.
*   **Natural Language Processing (NLP):** Teaching computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language.
*   **Robotics and Autonomous Systems:** Designing intelligent agents that can perceive and act upon the physical world.
*   **Data Science and Engineering:** Focusing on large-scale data analysis, data mining, and information retrieval.
*   **High-Performance Computing:** Building the powerful systems necessary to train and deploy large-scale AI models.

# Admissions Information

---

We welcome applications from motivated students who are passionate about the future of artificial intelligence. Our goal is to build a diverse and talented cohort of innovators from a wide range of academic and professional backgrounds.

### Application Requirements

A complete application consists of the following components, submitted through The Graduate College's application portal.

#### 1. Application Fee
*   **$55** nonrefundable fee for domestic applicants.
*   **$90** nonrefundable fee for applicants with international credentials.

#### 2. Transcripts & GPA
*   A copy of the official transcript from **each institution** where course credit was granted.
*   A baccalaureate degree from a regionally accredited university.
*   A minimum GPA of **2.75** (on a 4.0 scale) in your last 60 hours of undergraduate coursework.

#### 3. Test Scores
*   **GRE:** The Graduate Record Examination (GRE) is **not required** for admission to this program.
*   **English Proficiency Scores:** International applicants must submit an approved English proficiency exam score (e.g., TOEFL, IELTS, Duolingo) unless they have earned a degree from an accredited U.S. institution. Please see The Graduate College's website for a full list of accepted tests and minimum scores.

#### 4. Required Documents
*   **Resume/CV:** A current resume or curriculum vitae outlining your education, work experience, technical skills, and any relevant accomplishments.
*   **Statement of Purpose:** A thoughtful essay (typically 1-2 pages) outlining your personal and career goals and explaining why you want to pursue the M.S. in AI degree at Texas State University.
*   **Three Letters of Recommendation:** Letters should be from academic or professional references who can evaluate your skills, potential, and readiness for a rigorous graduate program.

### For Applicants from Non-CS Backgrounds

If you do not have a bachelor's degree in computer science or a related field, we strongly encourage you to apply. Our **[Pathways Program](./PATHWAYS-PROGRAM.md)** is specifically designed to provide you with the necessary foundational coursework to succeed.

### Diagnostic Interview

If you are accepted into the program, you may be invited to participate in a diagnostic interview with the graduate advisor. This interview is a supportive process to review your background and ensure you are placed in the appropriate courses to begin your degree program successfully.

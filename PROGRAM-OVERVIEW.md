# Program Overview

---

Texas State's Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence is more than a degree; it's a launchpad into one of the most transformative fields of our time. Our program is meticulously designed to provide students with a deep, practical, and ethically-grounded understanding of AI, preparing them for high-impact leadership roles in industry and research.

### Why Study AI at Texas State?

We offer a unique combination of cutting-edge curriculum, strategic location, and world-class resources that creates an unparalleled learning experience.

*   **Cutting-Edge Curriculum:** Our curriculum is focused on the most relevant areas of AI today. You will gain a deep understanding of **generative AI, machine learning, data analytics, and ethical AI applications**, blending foundational theory with the advanced, hands-on skills that employers demand.

*   **Strategic Location:** Situated at the heart of the **Austin-San Marcos innovation corridor**, our program connects you directly to a vibrant tech ecosystem. This proximity offers unparalleled internship and career opportunities with global tech giants like **Apple, Tesla, Dell, and Oracle**, as well as a thriving community of innovative startups.

*   **World-Class Facilities:** You will learn and conduct research in state-of-the-art facilities. In Fall 2026, the department will move into a new **$137 million, eight-story STEM building** designed to foster collaboration and innovation. Crucially, all MS in AI students have access to a dedicated, high-performance **multi-GPU server**, providing the computational power needed for complex deep learning projects.

*   **Exceptional Career Outlook:** The demand for AI expertise is exploding. The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects a **26% growth** for AI specialists and computer and information research scientists. Our graduates are prepared for high-demand careers as an **AI Specialist, Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, or Generative AI Developer** in diverse sectors like technology, healthcare, finance, and manufacturing.

### Our Vision: To Shape the Future of AI

The mission of the M.S. in Artificial Intelligence is to develop the next generation of leaders and innovators in the field. We provide a rigorous education in the theory, design, and application of intelligent systems, with a core focus on ethical considerations. Our program equips students with the advanced technical knowledge and practical problem-solving skills needed to create impactful solutions and drive technological advancement for the betterment of society. We aim to be a premier program recognized for producing graduates who are highly sought after by industry and top doctoral programs.
